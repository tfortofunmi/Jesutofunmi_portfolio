About Me

Get to know me

My name is Olowoniyi Jesutofunmi. I’m a Computer Science student who can’t seem to stay in one lane; my skills cut across design, code and everything in between. When I’m not programming, you’ll find me practicing UI designs and Illustrations, getting lost in Figma, or perfecting something in Canva. I know my way around Notion, Google Workspace and Microsoft Office (the kind of proficient where people ask me to fix formulas)

In my free time? I’m either scrolling through the internet or bulking up on my financial literacy because I’m so eager about wealth acquisition. I communicate well, both in writing and speaking. I can write clean documentation, present ideas clearly and explain complicated stuff in ways people understand.

I’m ambitious, organized and I learn fast; really fast. Put me on something I’ve never touched before and I’ll figure it out in a few hours. I work well with people and always open to freelance projects and collaboration.
